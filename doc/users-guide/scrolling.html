Chapter 3. Editing Text

Scrolling

View>Scrolling>Scroll to Current Line (shortcut: Control-E Control-J) centers the line containing the caret on the screen. View>Scrolling>Center Caret on Screen (shortcut: Control-E Control-I) moves the caret to the line in the middle of the screen.

View>Scrolling>Line Scroll Up (shortcut: Control-') scrolls the text area up by one line, without moving the caret. View>Scrolling>Line Scroll Down (shortcut: Control-/) scrolls the text area down by one line, without moving the caret.

View>Scrolling>Page Scroll Up (shortcut: Alt-') scrolls the text area up by one screenful, without moving the caret. View>Scrolling>Page Scroll Down (shortcut: Alt-/) scrolls the text area down by one screenful, without moving the caret.

View>Scrolling>Synchronized Scrolling forces scrolling performed in one text area to be propogated to all other text areas in that view. Invoking the command a second time disables the feature.
