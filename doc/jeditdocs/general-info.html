Chapter 1. General Information

Thanks for obtaining a copy of jEdit, a text editor written in Java. I hope you find it useful.

jEdit is released under the GNU General Public License, a copy of which can be found in the COPYING.txt file of the jEdit distribution.

jEdit comes with ABSOLUTELY NO WARRANTY OF ANY KIND; see section 11 and 12 of the GPL for details.

The jEdit homepage, located at http://www.gjt.org/~sp/jedit.html contains the latest version of jEdit, along with plugin downloads.

There are three mailing lists dedicated to jEdit; an announcement list that is very low traffic, a general discussion list, and a development discussion list. To subscribe, unsubscribe or view list archives, visit http://www.sourceforge.net/mail/?group_id=588.

Finally, you may contact me directly by e-mailing <sp@gjt.org>.
