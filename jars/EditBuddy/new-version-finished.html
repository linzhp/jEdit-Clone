Further information

You can view a detailed list of what's new in this jEdit release by clicking the button labelled Detailed Change Log.

You can view a summary of what's changed since the last major release by clicking the button labelled Overview of Changes.

If you would like to keep up-to-date in the world of jEdit, you should subscribe to one of the mailing lists. More information is available from the jEdit web site, located at http://jedit.sourceforge.net.
